Privacy Terms

At WashyWash, your privacy is our priority. We collect only the information necessary to provide you with the best laundry service experience. Your data will never be shared with third parties without your consent.

What Information We Collect

  • Your name, address, and contact details for pickup and delivery.
  • Order details, such as laundry preferences and special instructions.
  • Payment information for processing transactions securely.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our laundry services.
  • To communicate updates about your order.
  • To ensure secure and reliable payment processing.

For more details, please review our full privacy policy in the app or contact us at support@washywash.com.
