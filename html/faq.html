Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about WashyWash and our services.

WashyWash is a mobile app that connects you with trusted laundromats in your area. Simply place an order through the app, schedule a pickup, and we’ll handle the rest. Your clean laundry will be delivered back to your doorstep.

We offer washing, drying, ironing, and folding services. You can customize your order based on your needs. We also provide real-time tracking of your laundry order.

You can pay securely through the app using your credit card, debit card, or other supported payment methods. All transactions are encrypted for your safety.

WashyWash currently serves major cities and their surrounding areas. Check the app to see if we’re available in your location.

You can add special instructions when placing your order in the app. Our team will ensure your preferences are followed.
